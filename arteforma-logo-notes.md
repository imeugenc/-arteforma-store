# ArteForma Logo Concepts

Brand direction: premium, modern, custom fabrication, 3D print, bespoke objects.

## Concept 1

Sculptural AF monogram.
The diagonal structural line suggests layered fabrication and precision builds.

## Concept 2

Sharper and more technical.
Feels closer to engineered geometry and custom production.

## Concept 3

Luxury badge direction.
Best if you want ArteForma to feel more like a premium studio brand than a technical workshop.

## Recommended palette

- Gold: `#B68B4C`
- Deep gold shadow: `#8B6934`
- Black: `#0B0B0D`

## Recommendation

If the brand should feel premium and still clearly connected to making things, Concept 1 is the strongest starting point.
