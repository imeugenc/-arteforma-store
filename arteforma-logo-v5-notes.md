# ArteForma Logo v5

Direction:

- premium wordmark first
- small secondary symbol
- cleaner and more mature than a forced monogram
- black and gold
- motto: `Made Different`

Files:

- `arteforma-logo-v5-wordmark.svg`: clean vector logo on transparent background
- `arteforma-logo-v5-lockup-on-black.svg`: dark presentation version
- `arteforma-logo-v5-symbol.svg`: small supporting symbol

Design intent:

This version treats `ArteForma` as the hero and keeps the symbol understated.
The result should feel closer to a high-end product brand than a flashy gaming or automotive mark.
